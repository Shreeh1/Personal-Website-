• Dandeli

    "Let me start my first blog post with this trip, though previously we had been on multiple travel and adventures this is always a special one as I don't think we will be able to travel the same way we did back in 2012.( You will know why I am telling this as we reach the end of the travel blog ) okay let's start this...

    We were done with our 12th board exams and most us knew "man 12th got fucked up" and we really wanted to travel, so obviously budget was an issue though we wanted to travel to North India (which we did one year later lol) so finally we layed a travel map so the plan was we should kickstart the trip from Goa and then go to dandeli, from dandeli we had no idea what the plane was. So we were all packed and set to leave to Goa on a train journey all excited. It was monsoon then, we reached vasco. By the time we loaded our bags into the taxi we were already kind of drenched (still the first time in the trip out of the million times we were wet :P reached Calangute, damn excited that we were in Goa but back then we were hmmm what's the word, yeah "naive" lol so Goa wasn't for us ...as the day went by we were bored, so decided to leave Goa the very next day and left for Dandeli. We were in aww with the Lucious thick forest, the weather, the forest department dorm that was home for the next 2 days. Dandeli has its own charm during monsoons, the very next we were beside the river kali, and did a whole lot of adventure activities right from river crossing, tree hiking, kayaking and went on a hike in those thick forest, all drenched, then returned to our dorm and the food which I still remember. The same homely food was enough to get us going for many many days in the forest. Again in the night we decided to go for a hike, which is strictly prohibited and it's an offence which can get you behind bars. But we being teenagers, with torch lights we started the hike, it was called the birds nest. We hiked for a while, like 15 mins into the hike the route ended so did the hike lol and that was the last day in dandeli . The very next day we were in shimoga, place with highest rainfall in Karnataka, as it was monsoon the place looked surreal, and jog falls which is the most scenic place according to me during monsoons was lit. Again irritatingly drenched all day, and most irritating was to sit in those wet clothes in the bus and travelling miles to reach the next place. Finally the next morning just seeing the sun all cozy and warm was the best feeling ever. We had our moment with the sun :P . Again with our backpacks we were trekking to the beautiful sathodi waterfalls. The first visual of the falls with a rainbow and that was enough for us to right away jump into the falls and the next 3 hours went pretty fast. After lunch and some rest we headed to chikmaglur which was not the part of the plan ..but paisa abi bhi thoda bacha tha lol so hopped on to the bus and reached chikmaglur. Hired a taxi and we were off to mulayangiri, and not soo surprisingly this time it was heavy wind with rain even the cars parked were shaking. But still we had our own moments and memories there at the hill top. Almost 2 weeks into the trip now we had to go back home as our pockets were almost empty. This trip precisely was unorganized yet one of those memorable trips where luxury of travelling din't matter, the places we stayed din't matter, the rain din't matter, the mode of transportation dint matter and overall the only thing that mattered was to enjoy in the moment with no complaints. So it's important to be on one such trips where nothing materialistic but only travel and adventure matter."
